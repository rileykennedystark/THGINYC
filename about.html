ABOUT THE HORNIEST GIRLS IN NEW YORK CITY


This is a story of hope, redemption, power, and friendship.

It’s about two girls and their quest to save the world. And fighting the mayor.


The Horniest Girls in New York City was invited to premiere at Edinburgh Festival Fringe, the world’s largest performance arts festival.

The show will debut July 31–August 11, 2025 at Underbelly Cowgate, where Phoebe Waller-Bridge’s Fleabag first debuted to the world as a one-woman show.
