ABOUT THE HORNIEST GIRLS IN NEW YORK CITY


This is a story of hope, redemption, power, and friendship.

It’s about two girls and their quest for world domination.


It is 1984.

Tess and Riley have let the world bend them over a table for all of their lives.

A series of unfortunate events sends the girls spiraling straight to rock bottom, forcing them face to face with reality for the first time in their lives, deep in the pits of being in terrible, terrible debt to the tax man.

A stroke of grace leads them to the office of Rabbi Adelstein, a wise Bostonian lesbian, who asks the girls to consider whether they’d like to remain asleep at the wheel.

No, say the girls. We’d like to fuck the world back.

Thus begins their journey to rally the gay, horny youths to take over New York City, so they can save the world. But first they must defeat Enemy #1: Jake, The Mayor of New York City.

This is the story of the Horniest Girls in New York City, and their quest for world domination.


The Horniest Girls in New York City was invited to premiere at Edinburgh Festival Fringe, the world’s largest performance arts festival.

The show will debut July 31–August 11, 2025 at Underbelly Cowgate, where Phoebe Waller-Bridge’s Fleabag first debuted to the world as a one-woman show.
