ABOUT

The Horniest Girls in New York City is a story of hope, redemption, power, and friendship.

It is about Tess and Riley, and their quest for world domination.
The Horniest Girls in New York City was invited to premiere at Edinburgh Festival Fringe, the world’s largest performance arts festival.

The show will debut July 31-August 11, 2025 at Underbelly Cowgate, where Phoebe Waller-Bridge’s Fleabag first debuted to the world as a one-woman show.

About Riley

Riley Stark was born in Nantucket, Massachusetts and grew to be 5 foot 8.

Riley is a writer based in Brooklyn, New York. She is currently in the second semester of giving herself an MFA in creative writing at ‘Riley University,’ which she named after herself.

Riley is a substitute teacher for the New York City public school system, and she hopes to teach 9th grade English and Reading after she finishes her writing studies.

Riley is the oldest of 5 siblings, captain of a gay volleyball team, and she is very good at taking care of chickens. Her favorite books are ‘For Whom The Bell Tolls’ by Ernest Hemingway and ‘Paul Takes The Form of a Mortal Girl’ by Andrea Lawlor.

Riley is not known for her writing, yet. But she is writing this play.

About Tess

Tess Lancaster was born in Walpool, Massachusetts and grew to be 5 foot 8.

Tess is an actor, writer, and producer based in Brooklyn, New York. She graduated from New York University, then wrote and directed musical theater shows for elementary schools across Manhattan for 3 years.

Tess is now pursuing parallel careers in accessibility and entertainment as a recently certified Accessibility Coordinator.

Tess is best known for her two year run as a host for the popular TikTok and YouTube account “Thisisthetake”. Her work can be seen on The Discovery Channel, Amazon, and in film festivals across the US. Her newest projects will be featured in the upcoming “Funny Under 15” and “ReelAbilities” film festivals in New York this March and April.

Tess is also writing this play.

Theater & Comedy.

Riley Stark and Tess Lancaster.

SYNOPSIS

This is a coming-of-age story about two girls realizing they have agency over the choices they make after years of letting life, boys, drugs, and Tess’s cult past bend them over a table.

It is 1984, and a series of unfortunate events send the girls spiraling toward rock bottom at an inextinguishable pace, forcing them face to face with reality for the first time in their lives, all the way down in the pits of being $5,000 in debt (approx. equivalent to $15,287.34 in 2025).

A stroke of grace leads them to the office of Rabbi Adelstein, a wise Bostonian lesbian who came of age herself at the dawn of the United States Gay rights movement. She asks Tess and Riley to consider what on earth they’ve been doing with their lives, and whether or not they’d like to remain asleep at the wheel.

No, say the girls. We’d like to fuck the world back.

Thus marks the start of the girls’ quest for world domination, as they embark on a journey to learn how to harness the full potential of their power.

MANAGING COMPANY

ABOUT THE MANAGING COMPANY

Toonieroonie Productions is a comedic animation company based in Brooklyn, New York. Owners Tess Lancaster and Riley Stark, who independently perform sketch comedy and improv, expanded the company this year to include stage performance. Toonieroonie’s first performance of The Horniest Girls in New York City was held on March 14th with The Village Salon in Manhattan. This will be the company’s first time at Fringe (exciting!).
