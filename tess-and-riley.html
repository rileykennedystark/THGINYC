The Horniest Girls in New York City is written by and starring Riley Stark and Tess Lancaster.

About Riley

 Riley Stark was born in Nantucket, Massachusetts and grew to be 5 foot 8.

Riley is a writer based in Brooklyn, New York. She is currently in the second semester of giving herself an MFA in creative writing at ‘Riley University,’ which she named after herself.

Riley is also the oldest of 5 siblings, captain of a gay volleyball team, and a substitute teacher for the New York City public school system.

Riley is writing, producing, and starring in The Horniest Girls in New York City.

About Tess

 Tess Lancaster was born in Walpool, Massachusetts and grew to be 5 foot 8.

Tess is an actor, writer, and producer based in Brooklyn, New York. She graduated from New York University, then wrote and directed musical theater shows for elementary schools across Manhattan for 3 years.

Tess’s work can be seen on The Discovery Channel, Amazon, and in film festivals across the US, including “Funny Under 15” and “ReelAbilities”.

Tess is also writing, starring in, and producing The Horniest Girls in New York City.
